\section{List with xmref}
\label{sec:xmref:other}

In \autoref{sec:basic example} following labels were defined:
to which we can refer with \verb|\xmref|:
\begin{center}
\begin{tabular}{|l|l|l|l|}
\hline

\hline

\verb| \xmref{ht:pyth}|            & \xmref{ht:pyth}     &                                          &  \\
