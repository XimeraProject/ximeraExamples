At this point, we use MathJax to display math.

With \verb|\[ ... \]| :
\[
\frac{\sin^2(x^2+\pi)}{\sigma + 1}
\]

With \verb|$$ ... $$| :
$$
\frac{\sin^2(x^2+\pi)}{\sigma + 1}
$$

With \verb|$ ... $| :
$
\frac{\sin^2(x^2+\pi)}{\sigma + 1}
$


There may be problems with \verb|\d| :
\[
\int f(x) \d x
\]


Also, arrays look better than tables.
